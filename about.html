About Hi, I'm Vridhi

Creative Solutions.
Clear Insights.
Clean Code.

I'm a tech-driven problem solver with a focus on data analysis and web development. I create clean, responsive websites and tech solutions that solve real business problems. With a strong eye for detail, I blend functionality with design and occasionally channel my creativity through content writing and graphic design.

How I Got Here

As a kid, I was always drawn to patterns whether in games, stories, math, or logic. That curiosity naturally led me into the world of technology, where solving problems felt just as thrilling as discovering hidden connections. Over time, what started as a fascination grew into a passion.

I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence, and graduated with honors in Data Science and Analysis. Along the way, I built projects, explored real-world applications, and discovered how tech can be both smart and meaningful.

Now, I get to do what I love: build responsive websites, analyze data to uncover insights, and design solutions that blend logic with creativity.

My Values & Beliefs

Curiosity Fuels Growth

Since childhood, I've asked why before how. That curiosity hasn't just stayed with me it’s what led me into tech, AI, data, and design. I believe asking the right questions opens doors to the right solutions.

Built with Intent

Every website I build or product I analyze isn’t just about function it’s about meaning. I care about how people feel when they use something I’ve worked on. Good design solves problems. Great design connects people.

Learn, Unlearn, Repeat

My academic background in Computer Engineering, AI, and Data Science gave me strong foundations. But real growth came when I stepped beyond them. I believe in staying teachable, especially in a field that evolves every day.

Creativity Belongs Everywhere

Even in logic, there’s imagination. Whether I’m coding, analyzing data, or designing something visual, I bring creativity to the table. It’s not a separate skill it’s how I approach problems, people, and possibilities.

Why Work With Me

When I take on a project whether it’s building a product, analyzing user journeys, or just untangling the chaos behind the scenes I dive in fully. I like understanding the “why” behind things, not just ticking boxes. I enjoy solving problems that matter, improving what already exists, and making sure every piece fits with intention. I’m curious by default, and detail-oriented by choice. I work fast, but I never rush what’s important.

Behind all of that is a mindset rooted in accountability. I believe in doing the work properly, communicating clearly, and staying honest even when it’s inconvenient. I don’t cut corners, and I don’t disappear when things get tough. I care about people I work with how they feel, how they’re impacted, and how well we move forward together. That’s how I show up: with consistency, clarity, and care always.
